Overview

The University of Yaoundé I (Université de Yaoundé I) is Cameroon's largest and most prestigious university, established in 1962. Located in the nation's capital, it serves as the premier institution for higher education and research in Cameroon and Central Africa.

Admission Requirements

Academic Requirements

  • High school diploma (Baccalauréat) or equivalent
  • Minimum GPA of 3.0/4.0 or equivalent
  • Passport and birth certificate
  • Academic transcripts for last 3 years

Application Process

  • Online application submission
  • Entrance examination (concours)
  • Interview process for competitive programs
  • Medical fitness certificate
